Our Story

About Royal Furniture House

Founded in the heart of Karachi Market, Royal Furniture House has spent over 12 years redefining how Pakistani families experience their living spaces. We believe furniture is more than function — it is an expression of identity, heritage, and lasting quality.

Every piece begins with the finest solid hardwood — sheesham, oak, teak, and walnut — hand-selected by our master craftsmen. Our artisans blend traditional Pakistani woodworking heritage with modern precision, ensuring every joint and finish meets an uncompromising standard of excellence.

We specialize in fully custom orders: from a single statement chair to a complete villa fit-out. No vision is too ambitious for our dedicated team of 40+ craftsmen.

Grade-A solid hardwood only — no MDF
1-year craftsmanship warranty on all pieces
Free in-home measurement & design consultation
Free delivery across Karachi on orders above PKR 50,000
